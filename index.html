Al-Aqarat Sales Point System

A sales point allows you to sell your units directly to potential customers. A sales point on a real estate system can also make it easier for agents and brokers to manage and track sales activities, such as available units, booking units, monthly/weekly/daily reports, and processing transactions. In addition, having a sales point on a real estate system can help agents and brokers track and analyze customer behavior, which can help understand what types of properties are most popular and identify potential areas for improvement. This can help agents and brokers optimize their sales and marketing efforts and increase overall sales.
